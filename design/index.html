Elevation & motion

Cards are flat by default (hairline, no shadow). The signature elevation is the hard, blur-less green offset — the "machined" shadow — reserved for the primary action. Motion is quick and purposeful (150–300ms, ease-out).

Do / Don't

✓ Do
  • Protect the one-accent rule
  • Lead with numbers, bold-italic
  • Uppercase-wide chrome, sentence-case Inter prose
  • Dim imagery under a scrim
  • Machined shadow on the primary action
  • Reuse the status object everywhere
✕ Don't
  • Add a second brand hue
  • Use status colors decoratively
  • Put white text on green
  • Use emoji or hand-drawn icons
  • Stack textures or gradient-wash backgrounds
  • Round-corner + left-accent “card slop”
  • Synthesise a heavier Chakra Petch weight
